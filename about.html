Meet the founders of Exxion

Mechanical Engineer

John Matthew Makanthaw graduated with honors from Stanford University with a degree in Mechanical Engineering. He specialized in satellite engineering for private companies, contributing to the advancement of space technology. John played a pivotal role in the successful launch and operation of multiple space technologies, pushing the boundaries of space exploration. John continuously sought solutions to engineering challenges, displaying a creativty and forward-thinking skills. In 2021 John decided to create a company of his own, Exxion

John Makanthaw

Exxion Founder

Robotics Software Engineer

Jason Bradley completed a degree in Robotics Software Engineering at Georgia Institute of Technology, honing skills programming. He had a succesful career at Boston Dynamics, where he contributed to the development of cutting-edge robotic technologies. He met John Makanthaw at a Space conference, where Makanthaw's visionary ideas about space technology captured his imagination. Inspired by Makanthaw's vision, Jason decided to transition from robotics to space technology, driven by the potential to contribute to an incredible frontier.

Jason Bradley

Exxion Co-Founder

Aerospace Engineer

Christopher Hollenstein graduated from Purdue University, specializing in Aerospace Engineering. He contributed his expertise to SpaceX, actively participating in groundbreaking space missions. Christopher gained invaluable hands-on experience in designing, testing, and launching rockets, solidifying his position as a skilled aerospace engineer. A long-time admirer of Makanthaw's work,
Christopher coincidentley met him years ago they exchange numbers until Mackanthaw contacted him with the idea of Exxion.

Astronomer

Mark Terrence graduated with distinction in Astronomy from Yale University, nurturing a strong foundation in deep celestial sciences topping his astronomy class showcasing mastery. Mark contributed his expertise to NASA, accumulating extensive insights in space exploration and research. He previously collaborated with Mackanthaw on a successful project, showcasing good teamwork skills. Impressed by his teamwork skilss and capabilities, Mackanthaw invited Mark to join forces for the innovative company that exists today as Exxion.

How and we started our company Exxion

The start of Exxion

In a meeting orchestrated by John Makanthaw, the visionary founder of Exxion, a convergence occurred in 2022 between Jason Bradley, a robotics expert; Christopher Hollenstein, a seasoned SpaceX engineer; and Mark Terrence, an astronomer with NASA experience. United by their shared passion and fueled by Makanthaw's trailblazing reputation, this quartet embarked on a journey to shape the future of space technology. Their reunion, a culmination of years of admiration, marked the catalyst for the fusion of robotics, aerospace, and astronomy expertise, all coalescing within Exxion's transformative satellites. This union bore witness to the emergence of a new era in deep space exploration, characterized by unprecedented innovation and a boundless commitment to reaching the stars like never seen before.

Our core values at Exxion

Innovation

At Exxion, innovation is the beating heart of our company. We believe that only by pushing the boundaries of what's possible can we truly unveil the mysteries of the universe. Innovation propels us beyond the known and into the uncharted, enabling us to redefine space exploration through groundbreaking technologies and visionary concepts. It's through continuous innovation that we can capture awe-inspiring imagery, pioneer new forms of communication, and develop advanced propulsion systems. Our commitment to innovation reflects our dedication to shaping the future of cosmos.

Community

Community is the foundation of Exxion's ethos. We understand that our collective efforts drive progress and enrich our journey. Nurturing a sense of belonging and collaboration among our team members, partners, and space enthusiasts fosters an environment where diverse perspectives thrive. Through community, we amplify our impact and cultivate a space exploration culture that inspires, educates, and empowers. Our commitment to community ensures that as we venture together, we remain united in the purpose of advancing humanity's understanding of the universe.

Logic

Logic is the guiding force behind Exxion's innovations. We recognize that rational thinking and evidence-based approaches are crucial for navigating the complexities of space exploration. Embracing logic allows us to design and develop visionary technologies with precision and efficiency. By grounding our endeavors in logical reasoning, we ensure that our projects are not only ambitious but also feasible. This commitment to logic ensures that our exploration efforts are well-informed, reliable, and capable of expanding humanity's cosmic horizons a thoughtful and systematic approach.

Creativity

Creativity lies at the heart of Exxion's endeavors, propelling us to redefine the trajectory of space exploration. We understand the transformative potential of imaginative thinking in crafting the future of our cosmic journey. By embracing creativity, we unlock the ability to envision novel solutions to complex challenges, infusing our projects with fresh perspectives and innovative approaches. Nurturing a culture of creativity within our team, partners, and enthusiasts ensures that our initiatives transcend conventional boundaries, resulting in impactful and groundbreaking outcomes.

At Exxion, our goal is leading innovation in space exploration. We employ a multifaceted approach, emphasizing collaboration, logic and creativity.
